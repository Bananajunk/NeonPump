🛠️ Improvised Iron Cheat Sheet

No gym? No problem. Use these universal swaps to keep the gains flowing when equipment is light.

  • No Barbell? Use heavy dumbbells. If you don't have those, use resistance bands anchored to a door.
  • No Bench? Do floor presses. Lay flat on the ground and press—it actually protects your shoulders.
  • No Cable Machine? Swap cable flyes/pushdowns for resistance bands or standard dumbbell equivalents.
  • No Pull-Up Bar? Do inverted rows under a sturdy table, or heavy dumbbell bent-over rows.
